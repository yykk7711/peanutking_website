Software Engineer

Responsibilities:

  1. Develop new algorithms to enhance performance of our products and design projects, e.g., robot kits, AI products and computer vision systems.
  2. Work with Teaching Team and Software team to design, implement, adjust and test the hardware of projects and flagship products.
  3. Benchmarking or algorithms and software performance of flagship products and projects.
  4. On-site code debugging for clients in projects if necessary.
  5. Prepare technical documents of software architecture and protocols of projects.
  6. Assist Teaching Team in STEM activities and projects to teenagers including material preparation and coaching.
  7. Implement your ideas and do R&D.

Experience & Requirements:

  1. Degree holder of Electronic Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Information Engineering or related fields.
  2. A team player with collaboration skills to work in projects.
  3. Passions in learning, teaching teenagers, sharing knowledge and experience.
  4. Being self-motivated and responsible to work and communicate in a team atmosphere.
  5. Strong background in C, C++, Python or Java programming.
  6. Bonus for relevant experience in mobile Apps development, e.g., iOS / Android Apps
  7. Bonus for relevant experience in robotics, AI, computer vision, embedded firmware or relevant fields.
  8. Bonus for relevant experience in local STEM education or teaching / coaching in local STEM activities.
  9. Bonus if possess or pursuing PGDE or related education certificates.
