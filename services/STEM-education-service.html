Robotic Soccer Training Course

Participants will receive certificates of completion upon finishing the course.

Students learn programming and robotics components through assembling the soccer theme based robot - Robot PILA, developed and produced in-house for this objective based course.

Target:

F.2-F.5 students

Suggested duration:

16 hours

Class Contents:

  • Introduction to soccer robot structure and robot assembly
  • Basic programming and circuit theory
  • Sensor testing and basic control
  • Rules of robotic soccer tournament
  • Robotic soccer friendly match

Teaching materials:

  • Robot PILA
