軟件工程師

職務:

  • Develop new algorithms to enhance performance of our products and design projects, e.g., robot kits, AI products and computer vision systems.
  • Work with Teaching Team and Software team to design, implement, adjust and test the hardware of projects and flagship products.
  • Benchmarking or algorithms and software performance of flagship products and projects.
  • On-site code debugging for clients in projects if necessary.
  • Prepare technical documents of software architecture and protocols of projects.
  • Assist Teaching Team in STEM activities and projects to teenagers including material preparation and coaching.
  • Implement your ideas and do R&D.

    • 相關經驗:

  • Degree holder of Electronic Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Information Engineering or related fields.
  • A team player with collaboration skills to work in projects.
  • Passions in learning, teaching teenagers, sharing knowledge and experience.
  • Being self-motivated and responsible to work and communicate in a team atmosphere.
  • Strong background in C, C++, Python or Java programming.
  • Bonus for relevant experience in mobile Apps development, e.g., iOS / Android Apps
  • Bonus for relevant experience in robotics, AI, computer vision, embedded firmware or relevant fields.
  • Bonus for relevant experience in local STEM education or teaching / coaching in local STEM activities.
  • Bonus if possess or pursuing PGDE or related education certificates.
