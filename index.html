About Us

Founded in 2017, we provide STEM education services to local schools.

Our team are experienced in teaching STEM and robotics.

Unlike ordinary STEM tutoring courses, We customized our teaching kits and materials inhouse.

We provided STEM education services for >8 schools in last year. Our innovation was recognized and

we are now an incubate under the HK Cyberport Incubation program.

Highlighted Product - Peanut King Soccer Kit

Peanut King Soccer Kit is specially designed for students who interested in robotics. Students could control the movements of the robot through programming and enables the robot to perform Robotic Soccer Match.

Every component is detaily designed by our engineers. Students can obtain hands-on experience in knowing electronics and mechanics during the assembly. Plus, they can learn basic coding technics.
