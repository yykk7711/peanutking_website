About Us

Founded in September 2017 by a group of young engineering enthusiastic talents, Peanut KING has been providing STEM education services as well as inhouse tailor-made materials to local schools. We aim at prompting robotics technology and providing professional solutions on technical designs to schools and students.

Vision

We Create. We Innovate. We Solve.

  • To create opportunities for schools and students to experience robotics technology through STEM education;
  • To innovate new designs and ideas on STEM and robotics education with inhouse product production;
  • To solve, with profession solutions and state-of-art technology, the education of local primary and secondary schools.

Mission

Deliver state-of-art professional technology and innovation solution to schools, initiating a bright future

Offer primary and secondary schools STEM education courses, so as to popularize robotics technology and programming techniques and to cultivate the interest of Hong Kong teenagers towards innovation and technology

Provide comprehensive product prototyping and pre-production enquiry services to satisfy any needs of the customer

Past Achievements

Long term partnership with one of the eight schools of the Enriched IT Programme launched by the Government

Incubate under the Hong Kong Cyberport Incubation programme

Provide STEM Education to over 20 Schools

Various courses as well as the materials required are tailor-made for each school.

Team up with the experienced

Our students has once represented Hong Kong to join various international contests
